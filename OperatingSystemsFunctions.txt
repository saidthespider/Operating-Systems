Windows 10 has the speech feature to allow you to talk to use functions in Windows.
You use the windows key + H to bring up the microphone.
Then you simply say what you want windows to do or to dictate text you want it to write.

By pressing the windows key + shift + S will open the snipping tool.
You can then click and drag with your cursor to take a "snip" of your screen.
It is then stored in the clipboard.
This a good way to quickly take large amounts of information down or log errors.

If you want to access a timeline of what you have been doing on Windows hit the windows key + tab.
This will allow you to scroll through and jump back to what you were doing.
You can search with keywords of look by date or time.
Settings and who can view can be changed from settings.
